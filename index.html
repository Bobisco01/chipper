Life at Chipper


We’re an African fintech proud to serve more than five million customers.
As a global startup, we work at a fast pace (we call it ‘Chipper speed’) across multiple
timezones to solve some of the toughest challenges in order to deliver on our mission:
‍to provide the most trusted and accessible financial services for people living in Africa
and beyond.




This means we place emphasis on high-performance, outcome-driven results and
adaptability. How we go about our work is just as important, and our Chipper values:
Be Customer Obsessed, Be Bold, Be an Owner, Be Communicative to Develop Trust—
guide how we show up for each other. We believe we do our best work by adopting a
hybrid approach; mixing remote with regular time spent together in one of our Chipper
offices dotted around the world.
